Train the system to recognize specific prescription images

How Training Works

Training mode allows the system to learn from specific images and provide exact outputs when those images are uploaded again in the future. This is especially useful for:

  • Specific doctor's handwriting that the system needs to recognize reliably
  • Common prescription formats used in your hospital or clinic
  • Testing and demonstration purposes

Note: Once you train an image, the system will always recognize that exact image and return the same results when it's uploaded again.
